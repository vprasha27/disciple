Services

Disciple Assistance is a classified based brand that supports International Students from all over the world migrating to Montreal in seeking high class education, bright future and settling in the Canadian environment.It is an collective platform that connects the real estate rentals sellers directly to the International students. Migrating to a new country is always a challenging part of life as a student.The very first question that came to mind when moving is “Where to stay…?? , Where will I live…..??”. For those who have some connections definitely get help but what about those who have to push themselves by their own in the burgeoning society. Some have to pay a lot for staying even for a few days. As all students are new and get trap in the brokerages and still didn't get that much support.So to overcome all these obstacles Disciple Assistance illuminates these challenges faced by the students.It connects the rentals owners and sellers directly to the students so that they can get a place of their choice within a cheaper price. It allows the users to post the advertisements and have the opportunity to connect.Not only rentals but it also deals in buying and selling the real estate properties Disciple Assistance is a local and environmentally beneficial approach to recycle items. Furthermore, because the connections are made between people who live nearby, you can always view, touch, and sample an item before you buy it..
